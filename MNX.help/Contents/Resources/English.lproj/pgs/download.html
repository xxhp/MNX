MNX Help

Download Data

When your Mainnav device is connected to your Mac, Mac OS X will see it as a modem. And an annoying window will appear, says, 'A new network interface has been detected'. Just click on 'Cancel' and ignore the message.

There is a pop-up button on the toolbar of the main window of MNX, the pop-up button lists all connected modems, and your GPS logger is one among them. Please select your device from the pop-up button, the name of your device may look like 'usbmodem20100111'.

When you are ready, just click on the "download" button, then MNX will start downloding data.
